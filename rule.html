General Rules & Guidelines to be adhered in the Institute

  • Identity card is compulsory
  • Polythene bags are not allowed
  • Helmet is necessary for two wheeler riders
  • Smoking & alcohol is strictly prohibited
  • Non-vegetarian food is not allowed
  • As per Kurukshetra University norms 75% minimum attendance is required
  • T-Shirts, Shorts, revealing attire or miniskirts and attire printed with offensive language or obscene wordings are not allowed
